1. Introduction

Welcome to Rozzi ("Platform," "App," "we," "our," or "us"), operated under the parent entity Niratma. Rozzi is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, store, process, protect, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application and related web services.

By using Rozzi, you consent to the data collection and practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with any terms of this Privacy Policy, please discontinue use of the Platform immediately.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, as well as metadata generated automatically when you interact with the app. This is classified as follows:

A. Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Profiles (Google Sign-In): When you register using your Google account, we collect your name, email address, profile photo, and unique Google ID token.
  • Job Seeker Specific Information: Resumes/CVs (stored securely on Cloudinary), educational details, professional history, work experience, specific skills, and job preferences. We also collect verification documents (such as ID cards) if submitted for profile verification.
  • Employer/Recruiter Specific Information: Company name, address, industry sector, contact numbers, corporate email, billing credentials (processed via Razorpay), and job posting descriptions.
  • Support & Communications: Messages, feedback, or bugs reported to our support emails (e.g., setunajobs@gmail.com).

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Logs: Search history, views on job listings, job application records, recruiter contacts, and activity timestamps.
  • Device & Network Data: IP address, device type, operating system version, mobile network carrier, unique device identifiers, and language preferences.

3. How We Use Your Data

Rozzi processes your data to deliver a modern, personalized, and safe job recruitment experience. Specifically, we use it for:

  • Job Matching and Placement: Facilitating connection between recruiters/employers looking for talent and job seekers seeking opportunities.
  • Account Authentication: Securely authenticating your login requests using secure JSON Web Tokens (JWT).
  • Payment Processing: Handling billing for employer premium plans or job advertisement spots securely via our payment processor, Razorpay.
  • Media Management: Hosting profiles, resumes, and documentation using Cloudinary.
  • Analytics & Improvement: Diagnosing app issues, monitoring system logs, and improving the app layout and match algorithms.

4. Data Sharing & Third-Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We only share your data with trusted partners and third-party service providers to facilitate app operations:

  • Between Job Seekers and Employers: When a Job Seeker applies to a position, their profile details, resume, and contact email are shared directly with the Employer.
  • Third-Party Integrations:
    • Cloudinary: For secure hosting and cleanup of profile images, resumes, and identification documents.
    • Razorpay: To process premium subscription payments. Your payment credentials (card details, UPI, banking credentials) are encrypted and processed entirely by Razorpay; Rozzi never stores your credit card details on its servers.
    • Google Cloud Console: For managing secure OAuth login sessions.
  • Legal & Compliance Requirements: We will disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to prevent fraudulent or illegal activity.

5. Data Security

We take security seriously. All network communication between the Rozzi mobile application and the Niratma backend is encrypted using **HTTPS / SSL (TLS 1.2+)**. User authentication is managed using secure, encrypted JSON Web Tokens (JWT). All media files (resumes/images) are stored on encrypted Cloudinary storage.

Important Notice: While we deploy industry-standard measures, no method of transmission over the internet or mobile storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security against unauthorized access or breaches.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal data for as long as you keep your account active. You have full control over your data. You may request account deletion at any time in the app profile settings. Upon account deletion:

  • Your profile details, education, resume links, and job applications will be permanently erased from our primary database.
  • Associated uploaded media (profile photos, resumes) will be queued for automated deletion on Cloudinary.
  • We may retain anonymized usage data or specific payment history logs solely for accounting, tax compliance, or audit requirements.

7. Children's Privacy

Rozzi is designed strictly for adults and professionals. We do not knowingly collect, request, or maintain data from individuals under the age of 18. If we discover that a minor under 18 has registered or provided personal information, we will delete it immediately from our servers.
