The Hiring Market is Broken.

Standard job boards are filled with outdated listings, consultancies, spam, and hidden charges. Here is how Rozzi changes the paradigm.

Traditional Job Boards

  • Endless consultancies collecting resumes rather than actual hiring managers.
  • Fake or ghost job postings to inflate candidate pools.
  • Third-party marketing spam sent to your personal email and phone number.
  • Hidden fees and commission models that take cuts of your placement salary.

The Rozzi Paradigm

  • Verified Direct Recruiters: Strictly no third-party consultancies. Connect directly with company HR teams.
  • Zero-Spam Commitment: Listings are active and auto-deleted once filled.
  • Secure Native Chats: Recruiters can only contact you in-app. Your phone number is never shared publicly.
  • Completely Free for Job Seekers: We charge a small transparent posting fee to recruiters, never taking money from candidates.
